    How to find a PBC Cycle from the home page

    1.    Go to the OHS Coaching Companion URL.

    2.    Tap Click here to login.

    3.    All currently registered PBC Cycles will show on the home page.

    4.    Tap on the PBC Cycle to see grades, or add/edit content, including videos.

    How to view an PBC Step video

    1.    Click the correct PBC Cycle on the home page.

    2.    Click View next to the PBC Step to see the video.

     

    How to add an PBC Step video

    1.    Click the correct PBC Cycle on the home page.

    2.    Click View next to the PBC Step from the list.

    3.    Tap Add New Video bar.

    4.    Browse to choose the file.

    5.    Enter the title in the Title text box.

    6.    Click Upload.

    7.    A progress bar will update as the file uploads automatically.

    8.    Wait for the file to encode. A message will tell you the encoding is in progress. The video will not appear until it has been automatically encoded.



    9.    Review the file when it is uploaded.

    10. Rotate the image, if necessary, by clicking Rotate.

    11. Tap the Add Comments bar to add text.

    12. Click Add Comment.
